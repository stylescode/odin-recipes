Orange Chicken

Description

Orange chicken is an American Chinese dish of fried chicken in a sweet orange-flavored chili sauce glaze.

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 4 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1/3 cup Cornstarch
  • 1/3 cup Flour
  • Salt
  • Oil

Orange Sauce

  • 1 cup Orange Juice
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 2 tbsp Rice Vinegar
  • 2 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 tsp Ginger
  • 1/4 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Red Chili Flakes
  • Orange Zest
  • 1 tbsp Corn Starch

Garnish

  • Green Onions
  • Orange Zest
